Clash Royale

Clash Royale is a freemium real-time strategy video game developed and published by Supercell. The game combines elements from collectible card games, tower defense, and multiplayer online battle arena. The game was released globally on March 2, 2016.

Clash Royale reached $1 billion in revenue in less than a year on the market. The game grossed $550 million in 2016, according to market intelligence company Sensor Tower, making it the third highest grossing mobile game of the year. In 2017, the game grossed $1 billion, making it the fourth highest grossing mobile game. In 2018, the game grossed $2.5 billion, making it the seventh highest grossing mobile game. In 2019, the game grossed $1.5 billion, making it the sixth highest grossing mobile game. In 2020, the game grossed $709 million, making it the tenth highest grossing mobile game.

Clash Royale is an online multiplayer game where players battle in real-time to destroy the opposing towers, with the aim to win crowns, trophies and glory. The game features four main game modes: 1v1, 2v2, Touchdown, and a new game mode called "Duels". A player wins a battle by destroying more towers than the opponent, or by destroying the opponent's "King's Tower", which grants an automatic three "crown" victory. At the start of each game, both players are given a "hand" of four cards from a "deck" of eight cards chosen by the player. Cards can be used to attack and defend. To play the cards, the player must have enough "elixir", one elixir automatically being replenished every 2.8 seconds (every 1.4 seconds during "Double Elixir", every 0.7 seconds during "Triple Elixir", and every 0.35 seconds during "Rage"). Once a card is placed, a new card from the deck is drawn to the hand.
