History

Clash Royale is a real-time strategy game that was released globally on March 2, 2016 by Supercell. It combines elements from collectible card games, tower defense, and multiplayer online battle arena. The game first softlaunched on iOS on January 4, 2016 in eight select countries. The game has been updated regularly with new cards, features and balance changes.

Rules

Clash Royale is a game where you assemble a deck of cards to deploy units and destroy your opponent’s towers. The matches last three minutes, and if both players have the same number of crowns (or none), the match goes into a two-minute overtime where the first tower destroyed wins. The main goal is to destroy the King’s Tower in the middle, while protecting your own. You can also join clans, chat with other players, and participate in special events.

Tips

There are many tips and tricks to improve your gameplay in Clash Royale. Here are some of the most common ones:

  • Be patient and don’t rush your attacks. Wait for the right moment to deploy your units and counter your opponent’s moves
  • Learn how to count elixir and keep track of your opponent’s elixir spending. This will help you gain an elixir advantage and plan your strategy accordingly
  • Know when to stop pushing and defend your towers. Sometimes, it’s better to let go of a tower and focus on defending the other ones or preparing for a counterattack
  • Create a versatile and powerful deck that can deal with different situations and threats. Try to balance your deck with different types of units, such as tanks, swarmers, splashers, spellers, etc
  • Don’t waste elixir or gems. Use them wisely to upgrade your cards, unlock chests, or buy special offers. Don’t spend them on random things or speed up chests that you can open for free
